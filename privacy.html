3. Facebook Login & Data

Our application uses Facebook Login to authenticate users. By using Facebook Login, you agree to Facebook's own Data Policy.

We only request the minimum permissions necessary to operate the application. You can revoke our access at any time from your Facebook account settings under Settings → Apps and Websites.

Revoking access on Facebook does not automatically delete data already stored in our system. To request full data deletion, visit our Data Deletion page.
