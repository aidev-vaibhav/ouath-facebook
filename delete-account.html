Delete Your Account & Data

You have the right to delete your data from our application at any time. This page explains how to remove all information we collected through your Facebook login.

How to delete your data

  • 1
    Revoke app permissions on Facebook

    Go to Facebook → Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites. Find user-management and click Remove. This immediately revokes our access to your Facebook account.

  • 2
    Submit a data deletion request

    Click the button below or email us directly. We will delete all your stored data within 30 days and send you a confirmation.

  • 3
    Receive confirmation

    Once deleted, you'll receive an email at your registered address confirming permanent removal of all your data from our systems.

Request Immediate Data Deletion

Click below to send a deletion request email. Include your Facebook account email or username so we can locate your records.
